## Company Profile

- **Name:** Voltaage
- **Website:** https://voltaage.io
- **Tagline:** "We don't guess. We compute."
- **What we do:** AI-powered decision platform for planning, deploying, and optimizing infrastructure networks. We combine geospatial intelligence, infrastructure data, usage patterns, and AI agents to analyze territories and predict demand — guiding investment decisions for better infrastructure placement, higher utilization, and improved return on capital.
- **Strategic direction:** Moving toward **Agentic GIS** — autonomous AI agents that don't just visualize data on maps, but actively identify optimal locations, predict demand, and recommend decisions.
- **Operational footprint:** France and Italy, 2,603 cities, 5,100+ active chargers analyzed
- **Backed by:** Techstars, EIT Urban Mobility, BPI France, AWS

## Pain Points to Detect

When evaluating a prospect, look for these signals:

1. **Infrastructure deployment challenges** — difficulty deciding where to build next
2. **Low utilization / demand forecasting failures** — assets underperforming expectations
3. **Coverage gaps** — incomplete network coverage, underserved territories
4. **CAPEX optimization needs** — overinvestment or underinvestment in infrastructure
5. **Grid constraint issues** — power availability, load balancing challenges
6. **Fleet electrification readiness gaps** — planning EV transitions without data
7. **Manual / spreadsheet-based planning** — using Excel or basic tools for infrastructure decisions
8. **Lack of geospatial intelligence** — decisions made without territorial data
9. **AFIR / regulatory compliance pressure** — EU Alternative Fuels Infrastructure Regulation deadlines
10. **Multi-site network optimization** — managing and expanding distributed infrastructure networks
