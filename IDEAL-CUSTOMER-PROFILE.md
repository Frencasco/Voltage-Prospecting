# Voltaage — Ideal Customer Profile

## Company Overview

Voltaage is an AI-powered infrastructure decision platform combining geospatial intelligence, infrastructure data, usage patterns, and AI agents. Products: SDIRVE Assistant (CPO profitability engine), Fleet Electrification platform, Agentic GIS.

## Technographic Signals

### Positive Signals (They Need Voltaage)
- Uses spreadsheets or manual processes for infrastructure planning
- Uses basic GIS (QGIS, Google Maps) without decision intelligence
- Has internal analysts but no scalable platform
- Mentions "data-driven" aspirations but lacks tooling
- Job posts for GIS analysts, infrastructure planners, or network optimization roles

### Neutral Signals (Could Go Either Way)
- Uses Esri/ArcGIS (may be entrenched but could add Voltaage as decision layer)
- Uses Carto or Mapbox (developer tools, not infrastructure-specific)
- Has consulting relationships for infrastructure strategy

### Negative Signals (Hard Sell)
- Built comprehensive internal infrastructure planning platform
- Recently signed enterprise GIS contract (within 12 months)
- No physical infrastructure network to optimize

## Negative ICP (Who NOT to Pursue)

- **Pure software companies** — No physical infrastructure to optimize
- **Companies with < 5 infrastructure sites** — Not enough scale for Voltaage's value
- **Companies with mature internal platforms** — High switching resistance, low pain
- **One-time project companies** — Need continuous intelligence, not one-off analysis
- **Companies outside infrastructure** — If they don't plan, deploy, or manage physical networks, Voltaage isn't relevant

## Buyer Personas

### "The Growth CPO CEO"
- **Role:** CEO or VP of Network Expansion at a Charge Point Operator
- **Goals:** Maximize ROI per station, expand network profitably, meet investor growth targets
- **Pain:** Every new station is a $50-200K bet. Bad locations lose money for years.
- **Motivation:** Prove to investors that expansion is data-driven, not guesswork
- **Message:** "Know exactly where your next station will be profitable before you break ground."
- **Channels:** LinkedIn, industry conferences, email

### "The Data-Driven Public Authority Director"
- **Role:** Director of Mobility or Infrastructure at a municipality/region
- **Goals:** Deploy public charging infrastructure efficiently, serve citizens, meet EU mandates
- **Pain:** Political pressure to deploy fast, but limited budget. Can't afford to waste public funds.
- **Motivation:** Demonstrate to citizens and regulators that public money is well spent
- **Message:** "Allocate public infrastructure funds where societal impact is highest. Data, not politics."
- **Channels:** Government events, LinkedIn, direct email, referrals from other authorities

### "The Fleet Transition Manager"
- **Role:** VP Operations or Fleet Manager at a company electrifying its fleet
- **Goals:** Transition fleet to electric with minimal disruption, optimize depot charging
- **Pain:** Complex logistics of fleet electrification — which vehicles, which depots, what charging infrastructure
- **Motivation:** Meet corporate sustainability targets while keeping operations running
- **Message:** "Electrify your fleet with confidence. Know where and when to deploy charging at every depot."
- **Channels:** Fleet management conferences, LinkedIn, industry publications
